FAQ Updates

26 February 2026

A new FAQ has been posted on the official Leviathans site, so I've updated the site to have the latest information. The most notable change to the site is that the two French battleships that were printed with standard dYellow torpedo slots on their cards but packaged with tokens showing HE hex patterns should follow the slots printed on the cards and use standard dYellow torpedoes rather than HE torpedoes. I had previously assumed that those were intended to be HE torpedoes to better match the tokens and the Poisson class destroyers.

Also, despite being published in late February of 2026, the FAQ is labeled as being from 30 November 2025. I've matched that date in the notes for elements that were impacted by the FAQ.

Search

30 January 2026

The preview version of the fleet builder has had the ability to search the component list for a couple of months now. That feature is now out of preview. It should make it easier to find and add specific items to your fleet.

Preview: Visual List

30 November 2025

I've been tinkering with the presentation of available components in the fleet builder. If you have preview mode enabled, you'll now get a different list of available components. Instead of just a list of names, each component now has a picture as well.

Since this is a bigger change, I'm interested in feedback before I move it out of preview. Does having images for the components make it easier to browse the list? Or do the list items taking up more space on the screen make it annoying to use? Let me know what you think of it.

Breach Numbers

28 November 2025

The pages for individual ships now include the breach numbers for their slots. I've had the data in the backend for a while, but they are now available to view on the generated pages. As always, let me know if you notice any errors in the data.

Copy Markdown

28 October 2025

The fleet builder has had the ability to show a markdown version of a fleet for a while now. That was intended to make it easy to copy-paste it to anywhere you wanted with formatting that would work well in apps like Discord.

I've taken that a step further now. There is now a button that will add the markdown version of your fleet directly to your clipboard without having to switch to the markdown view or select text. My hope is that this will be easier to use, especially on mobile devices.
