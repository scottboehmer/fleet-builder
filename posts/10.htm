FAQ Updates

26 February 2026

A new FAQ has been posted on the official Leviathans site, so I've updated the site to have the latest information. The most notable change to the site is that the two French battleships that were printed with standard dYellow torpedo slots on their cards but packaged with tokens showing HE hex patterns should follow the slots printed on the cards and use standard dYellow torpedoes rather than HE torpedoes. I had previously assumed that those were intended to be HE torpedoes to better match the tokens and the Poisson class destroyers.

Also, despite being published in late February of 2026, the FAQ is labeled as being from 30 November 2025. I've matched that date in the notes for elements that were impacted by the FAQ.
